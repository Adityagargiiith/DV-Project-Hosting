Description of Sunburst Chart Visualization

This sunburst chart visualization aims to depict the distribution of startups across various industry verticals and their corresponding sub-verticals.

  • Industry Verticals: The outermost ring of the sunburst chart represents different industry verticals, which are broad categories or sectors in which startups operate. Each sector is represented by a segment of the outer circle.
  • Sub-Verticals: Within each industry vertical segment, there are further subdivisions represented in the inner rings of the chart. These subdivisions, known as sub-verticals, provide more granular details about the specific focus or niche within each industry vertical.
  • Size of Segments: The size of each segment within the sunburst chart corresponds to the count of startups belonging to that particular combination of industry vertical and sub-vertical. Larger segments indicate a higher number of startups operating within that category.
  • Hoverability: On hover on any of the sector, it gives us the total percentage it contributes in the entire sector. The graph is clickable as well.

By visualizing the data in this hierarchical manner, the sunburst chart offers insights into the relative prominence of different industry verticals and their sub-divisions within the startup ecosystem. It helps viewers understand the composition and distribution of startups across various sectors and sub-sectors, providing a comprehensive overview of the startup landscape.
