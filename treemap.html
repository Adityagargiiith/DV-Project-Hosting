Description of Treemap Visualization

The Treemap Visualization illustrates the distribution of companies across cities in India, providing insights into their evaluations and concentration in different urban areas.

  • Treemap: The visualization utilizes a hierarchical layout where cities are represented as rectangles, with the size of each rectangle proportional to the total evaluation of companies located in that city. Larger rectangles indicate higher total evaluations, reflecting a larger number of companies or higher-valued companies.
  • Data Source: Data for this visualization is derived from a dataset containing information about companies and their respective cities in India. Each rectangle in the treemap corresponds to a city, with the area of the rectangle determined by the total evaluation of companies located within that city.
  • Hovering: Hovering over each rectangle in the treemap provides additional details, such as the name of the company and its evaluation. This interactive feature allows users to explore specific companies within each city and gain insights into their respective evaluations.

By visualizing company distribution across Indian cities in a treemap format, this visualization facilitates the identification of cities with significant concentrations of high-valued companies, offering valuable insights for analysis and decision-making in the context of India's startup ecosystem.
