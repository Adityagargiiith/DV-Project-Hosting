Description of Heatmap Visualization

This heatmap visualization illustrates the distribution of companies across different industries and years, providing insights into the evolution of the startup ecosystem over time.

  • Heatmap: The visualization presents a grid where each cell represents the number of companies in a specific industry joined in a particular year. The color intensity of each cell indicates the magnitude of company count, with darker shades representing higher counts.
  • Data Source: Data for this visualization is derived from a CSV file containing information about companies, including their industry and the year they joined. The dataset encompasses companies from various countries and cities worldwide.

By visualizing the distribution of companies across industries and years in a heatmap format, this visualization facilitates the identification of trends and patterns in the startup landscape, aiding stakeholders in making informed decisions and understanding the dynamics of the startup ecosystem.
